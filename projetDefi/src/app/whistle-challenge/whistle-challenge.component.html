Learn how to whistle in 10 hours!

You never achieved to whistle properly ? Try those ten easy steps to master this skill faster than you expected.



Day 1:

To achieve this challenge, you should try to start with the basics.
Position your lips as if you were trying to pronounce the "u" sound and blow gently some air to see if it creates a sound.
You don't need to try to whistle loudly at the begining. Just try to have this first whistle sound.

If you have it, keep it and repeat it!


When you feel you can reproduce this simple whistle with ease, you can then try to modulate the sound you produced moving your tongue inside your mouth.
It should create higher notes with your tongue up, and lower notes with the tongue down.

Practice during small sessions of 10 minutes during the day.
If you (or too many people around) you feel tired of it, take some time before going back to practice in better conditions!
